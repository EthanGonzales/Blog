Places Must Visit

Prithvi Cafe

I had visited in December 2018

The 92-seater Prithvi cafe is an open air cafe lined by big trees and bamboos. The place is renowned for its Irish coffee which has the perfect mix of froth and whiskey. The sandwiches and kebabs are equally yummy. This is an ideal place for a lazy Sunday afternoon when one wants to take a break from the maddening pace of the city and just relax in nature's bounty. This cafe is attached to the legendary Prithvi Theatre. The place is perfect to take your chess board, book or laptop and just relax over coffee.

Review on The Movies



Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.



Minions: The Rise of Gru
Decent Sequel to the Minions

I watched it once and I surprisingly enjoyed it. If you have watched at least the first 2 Despicable Me films and the first Minions, you may recognize some callbacks relating to those films. A few notable callbacks are young Nefario's appearance and the old ship. There are also a few references that relate to the 70's which is when the film takes place in. Such reference is a pet rock that was popular at the time. The animation is easily than the past films. It is a decent film to watch that children will almost certainly enjoy.
